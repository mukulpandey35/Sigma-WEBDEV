Kedarnath

Kedarnath is a holy pilgrimage site in the state of Uttarakhand, India. It is located in the Kedarnath Temple, which is one of the most revered Hindu temples in the Himalayas. The temple is dedicated to the Hindu god Shiva and is considered to be one of the holiest places in the world.
